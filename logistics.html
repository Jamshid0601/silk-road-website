Best Time to Visit

The climate of Uzbekistan is extremely continental. The best times to travel the Silk Road are during the spring (April to May) and autumn (September to October) when the skies are clear, and the intense summer heat of the Kyzylkum desert subsides.

Budget Guide

Uzbekistan is a highly affordable destination. A mid-range budget of $30–$50 per day easily covers comfortable guesthouse accommodation, domestic train travel, hearty local meals, and entrance fees to major historical sites.

Getting Around

The most efficient way to travel between the major Silk Road cities is the 'Afrosiyob' high-speed railway, connecting Tashkent, Samarkand, and Bukhara in a matter of hours. For Khiva, domestic flights or sleeper trains are recommended.
